# What Is It?

## Summary

**Run Towards Allah's Mercy** is an Islamic website that provides authentic duas and adhkar from the Qur'an and Sunnah. It is designed as a Sadaqah Jariah project so that people can continue benefiting from it even after the creator passes away.

## What It Is

- **A Sadaqah Jariah project** — Ongoing charity and beneficial knowledge
- **An Islamic duas and adhkar site** — Qur'anic duas and authentic hadith-based remembrance
- **A mobile-first reading space** — Calm, accessible design for reading on phones and tablets
- **An invitation to taubah** — Encourages turning back to Allah and seeking His mercy

## Who It's For

- Muslims seeking authentic duas and adhkar for daily use
- Readers wanting Qur'anic and hadith-based content with clear references
- Those who prefer Arabic with full harakat (diacritics) and English/Urdu translations
- Anyone looking for a focused resource for the last 10 nights of Ramadan and Laylatul Qadr

## Content Focus

The site focuses on:

- **Qur'anic Rabbana duas** — Supplications Allah placed in His Book
- **Authentic adhkar** — Morning and evening remembrance from reliable hadith sources
- **Last 10 nights** — Special duas and reflection for the most blessed nights of Ramadan

All content is Qur'an and authentic Sunnah only, with clear references so readers know what comes from the Qur'an and what comes from authentic hadith.
