# Run Towards Allah's Mercy — Wiki Home

## Overview

**Run Towards Allah's Mercy** is a Sadaqah Jariah Islamic website that provides Qur'anic duas, authentic morning and evening adhkar, and special content for the last 10 nights of Ramadan. It is a calm, mobile-first reading space for remembrance of Allah and spiritual reflection.

## Mission

The site exists to help people remember Allah, read authentic duas and adhkar, reflect on the guidance of the Qur'an and Sunnah, and keep benefiting from beneficial knowledge over time. It invites readers to do taubah and run towards Allah's Mercy.
